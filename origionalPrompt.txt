Project: CMAC Concierge – Internal Scheduling Concierge for CMAC Roofing
You are an expert AI web-app builder using Google AI Studio / App Builder, Google Calendar / Workspace APIs, and Google Gemini.

High-Level Concept
Goal:
Make it trivial for any CMAC employee to find a mutually available meeting time with a coworker, even when calendars are packed and users don’t know how to manually browse other calendars.
Core Idea:
• The app uses:
• Google Workspace authentication (OAuth).
• Google Calendar APIs (and optionally People/Admin Directory APIs).
• Gemini for understanding natural-language scheduling rules and generating smart suggestions.
• The app cross-references two users’ calendars (the currently signed-in user and another selected coworker) to:
• Find overlapping free time blocks.
• Apply user-defined constraints (duration, date range, days/times, etc.).
• Suggest times in a conversational, intelligent way.
• When the user confirms a time:
• The app creates a calendar event on the signed-in user’s calendar.
• Invites the other person as a guest.
• Optionally adds a Google Meet link.
• Uses Gemini to generate a clear, slightly creative meeting title and description based on the context.

Auth Method
• Use Google OAuth2 sign-in.
• Only allow users from the CMAC Google Workspace domain (e.g., @cmacroofing.com).
